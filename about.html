Meet the Friends

Every day they work hard pulling weeds, subjugating monsters, and enjoying tasty treats!

Chiikawa

Timid & Sweet

The main protagonist. Shy, gentle, and prone to crying, but incredibly brave when friends are in trouble.

Hachiware

Positive & Chatty

Chiikawa's highly optimistic cat-eared best friend. They live in a cave and loves to cook and play guitar.

Usagi

Eccentric & Energetic

A wild rabbit-like friend who screams "Yaha!" and "Ura!". Fearless, incredibly athletic, and highly intelligent.
